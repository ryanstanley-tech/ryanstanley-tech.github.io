Advanced CPU Overclocking & Silicon Tuning Project
This project showcases my ability to optimize an AMD Ryzen 9 7950X3D through advanced BIOS tuning, per-core Curve Optimizer offsets, and Precision Boost Overdrive adjustments. I focused on identifying golden and silver cores, managing Windows scheduler behavior between the cache and frequency CCDs, and balancing aggressive undervolts with long-term stability. Key challenges included hidden BIOS options, scheduler misalignment, and ensuring weaker cores did not limit stability. Through iterative testing with Cinebench, HWInfo64, and Ryzen Master, I achieved sustained boosts up to 5.7 GHz, stable CO offsets as deep as –27, and benchmark results that outperform most stock systems: 1942 in Cinebench R23 single-core and 1970 in Cinebench R24 multi-core, placing this CPU in the top 10% of its class.

SOHO Network Segmentation & Security Project
This project demonstrates the process of transforming a typical small office/home office (SOHO) network into a segmented, enterprise-style environment. Using a combination of a broadband gateway, managed switches, and network-attached storage, I designed and configured VLANs, routing, and security controls to separate personal, work, and lab traffic. Goals 1) Improve security by isolating devices into their own network zones. 2) Create a realistic lab environment for hands-on practice with professional networking and cybersecurity concepts. The setup includes proper VLAN segmentation, inter-VLAN routing, firewall rules, and shared resource access (e.g., a printer and NAS). All devices maintain internet connectivity while enforcing clear security boundaries. This mirrors how modern businesses secure and manage their networks, providing both practical functionality at home and a strong platform for certification study and professional growth.
